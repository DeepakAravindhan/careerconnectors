What is Digital Marketing?

Digital marketing is the use of online platforms and digital technologies to promote products, services, or brands to a targeted audience. It includes various strategies like search engine optimization (SEO), social media marketing (SMM), email marketing, and content marketing to reach potential customers through websites, social media, search engines, and other digital channels.

Elements of Digital Marketing

  • Search Engine Optimization – Improves website visibility on search engines through organic (unpaid) search results.
  • Search Engine Marketing – Uses paid ads on search engines to drive targeted traffic and boost visibility.
  • Social Media Marketing Promotes products and engages customers through social media platforms.
  • Content Marketing Creates and shares valuable content to attract and retain customers.
  • Affiliate Marketing Partners with affiliates who promote products and earn commissions for sales.

Job Openings in Digitl marketing

Entry-level positions such as Digital Marketing Executives typically earn between ₹3 – ₹6 LPA (Lakhs Per Annum) in India. Mid-level roles like Social Media Managers or SEO Specialists earn approximately ₹6 – ₹12 LPA, while Senior-level professionals such as Digital Marketing Managers or Strategists can earn ₹12 – ₹25 LPA. Experienced professionals specializing in advanced fields like Data Analytics, Performance Marketing, or Digital Marketing Consulting can command salaries exceeding ₹30 LPA.
